Nina Rappaport published the book of the same name in 2015 with Actar.


VERTICAL URBAN FACTORY was displayed at the Gallery at Industry City, Sunset Park, Brooklyn August through October 3, 2015. Vertical Urban Factory presented: Made in LIC June 25 at the Falchi Building in Long Island City, NY.

Vertical Urban Factory was displayed at Archizoom, the gallery of EPFL in Lausanne, Switzerland, March 23 through May 9, 2015.

The project was exhibited at:

  • Skyscraper Museum in New York City, 2011.
  • NYU's East Asian Studies Department with new material on globalization and its impact on labor and architecture in East Asia, Spring 2012.
  • Museum of Contemporary Art Detroit, Spring 2012.
  • Toronto Design Exchange Fall 2012.
  • Urban Space in Toronto in Spring 2013.
  • London's Museum of Architecture, King's Cross, Summer 2014.

Vertical Urban Factory features the innovative architectural design, structural engineering, and processing methods of significant factory buildings from the turn of the 20th century to the present. It provokes ideas for the future of urban manufacturing.

Future Scenarios are proposed based on new types of urban manufacturing:

  • Cleaner and Greener
  • The Transparent Factory
  • Local and Small
  • Living and Working
  • Vertical and Dense

See: www.verticalurbanfactory.org to make a contribution, watch a video, and learn more about the exhibition.
